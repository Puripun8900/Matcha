Popular Matcha Regions

Uji, Kyoto

Uji is Japan’s most famous tea region. Known for its ceremonial grade Matcha, Uji Matcha is rich, smooth, and deeply aromatic. It has been cultivated since the 12th century.

Shizuoka

Shizuoka Prefecture produces over 40% of Japan's tea. Its Matcha is vibrant green with a strong umami flavor, grown in the fertile valleys near Mount Fuji.

Nishio, Aichi

Nishio is famous for high-quality Matcha used in both culinary and ceremonial purposes. Its powders are finely ground and known for their vivid color and delicate sweetness.
